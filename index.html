ABOUT GMCA

Uniting Gospel Music Ministers Across Africa

Gospel Music Community Africa is a Christ-centred movement committed to raising worshippers, musicians, choirs, producers and creatives who serve God with excellence and integrity.

Through mentorship, collaboration, discipleship and practical training, we are building a generation whose music transforms lives and advances God's Kingdom.
